MYSIC SYSTEM

First step of learning the guitar is learning the chords. For playing guitar more seriously you need to learn scales, notes and tabs. Afterwards you will be capable to improvise and play solos, as well as learn other songs by your self without any tutorials.


NOTES

If you want to be professional musician, learn the notes

SCALES

Solos and improvisations require knowledge of scales

TABS

If learning notes is too challenging, try with easier option – tabs



Notes: Professional musicians learn notes. They allow you to know which tone you need to use to be able to play composition you want.


Tabs: Tabs have the same function as notes and they are easier for beginners to read. Tabs have six lines and they represent the guitar’s six strings (E, A, D ,G ,B ,E). The numbers on those lines represent which fret you need to press on which string to get the correct note.


Scales: There are many scales but for beginners the easiest one is ”pentatonic” scale. The will allow you to play solos and improvise in the right song key. For example, if song has G, A and D chord, you could play solo with G, A or D pentatonic scale. Pentatonic scale of each chord starts from the place where the the major note of scale is.
